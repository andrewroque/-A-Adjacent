[a] adjacent


[a] adjacent is a typographic exploration through processes of transfers. Letterforms bounce between the edges of physical and digital space creating new silhouettes in the process. Type is transferred from print to web, back into print creating iteration upon iteration of the original form. [a] adjacent examines the ongoing relationship between analog and digital spaces, studying the aspects of form that get lost in the process versus what gets added.
